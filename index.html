Our Story

About Budha's Detailing

At Budha's Detailing, every vehicle is treated with precision, care, and professional-grade products. Whether it's your daily driver, truck, SUV, sports car, or classic vehicle, our mission is simple: restore your vehicle's appearance and protect it for years to come.

Our commitment to quality and attention to detail ensures every customer drives away with a vehicle that looks and feels exceptional.

"We don't just clean cars — we bring out what red rock and desert light are supposed to reflect."
